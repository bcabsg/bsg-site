BSG Winter meeting 2024 - New Advances and Future Directions in Structural Biology

MRC-LMB Cambridge, Friday 6th December, Organisers: Andrew Carter (Cambridge) and Simon Newstead (Oxford)

Poster talks and prizes are available and will be selected from the submitted abstracts.
