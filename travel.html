Travel to the Royal Free Hospital Medical School


The Royal Free Hospital is located in the Hampstead / Belsize Park area of North London.

The closest overground station is Hampstead Heath, but it will be more convenient for most attendees to travel on the underground to the nearest tube station which is Belsize Park, on the Northern line.

Please make sure that you get on the Edgeware branch of the northbound Northern line - *not* the High Barnet branch!

A map of the area can be found by clicking here.

On leaving Belsize Park tube station, turn right and walk up the hill past the shops and restaurants. Keep going till you pass another row of shops with a pub on the corner. At this point turn right into Rowland Hill Street and the medical school entrance is at the bottom of the hill.

The Atrium lecture theatre is on the ground floor of the hospital and will be sign-posted.

More details of public transport in London, including maps, can be found by clicking here.

Parking at the hospital is limited and very expensive!!
