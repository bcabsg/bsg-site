Introductory Text Books

  • Outline of Crystallography for Biologists by David Blow (2002), 246 pages, Oxford University Press, ISBN 0198510519.
  • Crystallography Made Crystal Clear: A Guide for Users of Macromolecular Models by Gale Rhodes (1999), 256 pages, Academic Press, ISBN 0125870728.


Reference Works

  • Fundamentals of Crystallography (International Union of Crystallography Texts on Crystallography) by Carmelo Giacovazzo, H.L. Monaco, G. Artioli, D. Viterbo, M. Milanesio, G. Gilli, G. Zanotti, G. Ferraris, M. Catti. (2011), Oxford University Press.
  • Protein Crystallography by T.L.Blundell and L.N. Johnson. (1976), Academic Press.
  • Macromolecular Crystallography with Synchrotron Radiation by J. R. Helliwell (1992), Cambridge University Press.
  • Principles of Protein X-ray Crystallography by J. Drenth. (2007), Springer.
  • X-Ray Crystallography of Biomacromolecules: A Practical Guide by Albrecht Messerschmidt. (2007), Wiley.
  • Macromolecular Crystallography - conventional and high-throughput methods by M. R. Sanderson and J. V. Skelly. (2007), Oxford University Press.
  • Biomolecular Crystallography by B. Rupp. (2009), Garland Science.
  • Crystals, X-rays and Proteins: Comprehensive Protein Crystallography by D. Sherwood and J. Cooper. (2010, 2015), OUP. Corrigenda. 
  • Protein Crystallography Methods and Protocols by A. Wlodawer, Z. Dauter and M. Jaskolski. (2017), Springer.

Group Activities

Each year the BSG organises sessions at the BCA Spring meeting, organises a 1 day winter meeting and organises a protein crystallography summer school.
